Welcome To The Nostr Hyperspace

Overview

Definition: Nostr is an open protocol aspiring to establish a global, censorship-resistant "social" network.

Philosophy: It's resilient, tamperproof, and functional, seeking to avoid reliance on centralized servers or P2P techniques.

Progress: This project is currently in development, with discussions and collaboration occurring in a designated Telegram group.

Key Features

Client-based:

All users operate a client, which could be native or web-based.

Publishing involves writing, signing with a key, and sending posts to multiple relays.

Updates are fetched from multiple relays.

Relays:

Serve as servers, either self-hosted or hosted by others.

They forward posts from users without requiring trust; client-side verifies signatures.

A relay does not interact with another relay directly, only with users.
